*p*Surveyors order 64.93*/p*

*p*equal in cash to   ______________42.97                                  95.43*/p*

*p*Due in Cash_______253.07*/p*<barker_pb n="1149"/>

*p*Mr Lewis will pay the one half of the above to Jose Antonio

*p*San Felipe de Austin July 16. 1825*/p*

*p*Stephen F Austin*/p*

*p*[On back] Eecd of Robert Lewis One Hundred and twenty six
Dollars and [53 1/2] cts the amount of my proportion of the within
Acct. this 22d August 1825—*/p*

*p*Recibi del Sor Eoberto Lewis los ciento veinte y seis pesos 
sincuenta y tres y medio sueldos que me entrego por cuenta del Coronel
Esteban Austin.   Be jar 23 de Sepe, de 1825.*/p*

*p*José Ant°. Saucedo    [Rubric]*/p*

*p*Son 126 ps. 53 1/2 sueldos*/p*
